Its a canvas like thing, except made with html.
First, to get started, you import the script:

<script src="https://2kinc.me/critters/main.js"></script>

Then you make a world:

var world = new World(width, height, x, y)

Then you append the world!

element.appendChild(world.el);

To make a critter, you use new World.Critter(x, y, width, height, deg, innerHTML, name)

And a rectangle: new world.rect(x, y, width, height, degrees, color, name)
